  • At Least There is Čeda Čedović

RATING: ★★★★☆

A Super Mario World-esque platformer that pays homage to a ton of games. Suffers from some particularly annoying bosses and bad levels, but on the whole it's a solid platformer. Very funny cutscenes and fulfilling 100% which makes you want to complete it.

Genuinely one of the few times I've had a game had me go:
"No way I'm 100%'ing this, it's too hard.", then ended up coming back and beating it.
Really good music too. Well worth your time.

  • League of Piss

RATING: ★★★☆☆

Genuinely a fun point n' click + bullet hell game. Bosses are a huge highlight.
Keeps short and to the point if you know where to go, puzzles are confusing though.
Bosses are super fun and engaging, Clammy Pottel and Bean Weird are the best imo.

Pretty good 1-2 hour experience, worth a couple plays to see all it has to offer.
Made by Dany Burton, probably his best game so-far, keep an eye out for the sequel.

  • OMORI

  • Playthrough Here














RATING: ★★★☆☆

I've actually waited quite a long time for this game to come out, in the ball park of 4-5 years.
Not only that, but it kind of inadvertedly inspired some things in games like Gamit or WDA.
Honestly, because of that, I never really thought it'd come out nor did I think I'd actually like it.

But it's fun enough really, it's standard RPGMaker affair in terms of what was accomplished.
It falls into all the traps of an RPG of course, back-tracking, level-grinding, boring areas.
Bad pacing, etc etc... really I could go on...

But it kind of holds a special place for the time period it was originally supposed to come out in.
Also because the art, soundtrack, story, and everything else besides the gameplay is really good.
Do I particularly care? Not really..? But I don't dislike it either.

The routes and endings feel like cop-outs and I'm sure others have already ripped into it.
For other reasons, probably not just the gameplay.

I do think it is worth the play though so I won't say it's outright bad, it's just very very niche.
Alternatively, just watch ManlyBadAssHero's playthrough, probably the best one.
