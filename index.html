I am Durdge-o, amateur game developer and full time winner. I'm a big fan of fighting games, speedrunning, and source games. I've done a little bit of modding for plenty of games, even romhacking. But I mainly just play fighting games and work on games now. This website hosts my creations and other things I've done, as of right now it's mainly being used for art and games. It used to have some reviews but I took them down in favor of simplicity, maybe in the future I'll put fighting game stuff here? In the meantime though, you can read some blog entries or browse art. I started this as a project out of boredom, ended up morphing into my main hub for all my work. I've taken to keeping it with the hopes that it inspires others to try and make their own sites. Keep in mind that a lot of my work is from other html examples, this is just a personal website that shows those examples in use.
